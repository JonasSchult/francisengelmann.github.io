Biography

I am a PhD student in the Computer Vision Group at RWTH Aachen University supervised by Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe. My current research is focused on 3D scene understanding. In summer 2018, I interned at Apple 3D Vision Group. I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science from RWTH Aachen University in January 2014.
